My Memorable Trips and Experiences

I enjoy traveling and experiencing different cultures.Reflecting on the memories created brings me great joy and happiness.

Mombasa - Kenya Bangkok - Thailand Coming to America! Meeting Warren Buffet Hong-Kong and Shenzhen - China Wedding things - India

  • Mombasa - Kenya

    • I was born and raised in Nairobi, Kenya. A trip to the coast was always a welcome break from the hustle and bustle of city life. Here I am enjoying a camel ride on vacation in Mombasa.

  • Bangkok - Thailand

    • While living in Kenya, I worked for an airline and one of the greatest benefits was the opportunity to purchase cheap employee discounted tickets. A fun trip I took was to Bangkok. This is a picture I took at the airport upon my arrival.

  • Coming to America!

    • I migrated to the United States in January 2008 which is when I first experienced winter and snow. Here I am all bundled up!

  • Meeting Warren Buffet

    • St. Louis University is one of the business schools whose MBA class was invited to a day with Warren Buffet in Nebraska, Omaha. We had a Q&A session with him after which he treated us to lunch at his favorite restaurant - Piccolo's. The highlight of the day was getting to personally meet him and take a picture with him!

  • Hong-Kong and Shenzhen - China

    • As part of our International Business class during my MBA, we had the opportunity to study abroad in Hong Kong. Mornings were dedicated to class lectures, however we had the afternoons free. This was a great opportunity to tour the city. We visited various sites in Hong-Kong and also took a daytrip to Shenzhen.

  • Wedding things - India

    • An invitation to attend a friend's wedding in Goa-India was a great opportunity to vacation in India. I got to visit Mumbai, Goa and New Delhi while I was there.
